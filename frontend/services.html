Our Services

Comprehensive Care,
Tailored to You

From specialist nursing to joyful daily activities, every service we provide is designed around the individual — flexible, compassionate, and clinically excellent.

Core Services

Clinical Care Excellence

Our core care services are delivered by trained, experienced professionals committed to clinical excellence and person-centred care.

🩺 Core Service

Nursing Care

Led by highly qualified Registered General Nurses (RGNs), our comprehensive nursing service is available around the clock at Glan-yr-Afon. We meticulously assess, bespoke-plan, and oversee the complete wellbeing of every resident presenting with complex clinical requirements.

  • ✦ Complex nursing and medical needs
  • ✦ Wound management requirements
  • ✦ Post-operative recuperation
  • ✦ Frailty and limited mobility
  • ✦ Long-term chronic conditions
  • ✦ Specialist nursing diagnoses
🧠 Core Service

Dementia Support

Our dedicated teams receive advanced specialist dementia training to cultivate a profoundly safe, reassuring environment. Here, those living with dementia can sustain the highest possible quality of life. Our unequivocally person-centred approach focuses on truly understanding and celebrating the individual behind the condition.

  • ✦ Person-centred dementia care plans
  • ✦ Safe and stimulating environments
  • ✦ Life story and reminiscence therapy
  • ✦ Family involvement and support
  • ✦ Specialist trained dementia care staff
  • ✦ Regular clinical review and monitoring
🌿 Core Service

Palliative & End-of-Life Care

We possess deep-rooted experience in delivering extraordinarily compassionate palliative and end-of-life care. Working in seamless partnership with leading specialist services—including the Marie Curie Centre (Holme Towers), the St Davids Palliative Care Team, and local NHS trusts—we unequivocally ensure dignity, comfort, and absolute peace for residents and their loved ones.

  • ✦ Specialist palliative nursing care
  • ✦ Pain and symptom management
  • ✦ Family support and liaison
  • ✦ Spiritual and emotional support
  • ✦ Coordination with hospice services
  • ✦ Dignified end-of-life care environments
Daily Life Services

Enriching Every Day

Care isn't just about clinical support — it's about living a rich, fulfilling life. Here's how we make every day special.

🍽️
Freshly Cooked Meals

Our catering team prepares nutritious, freshly cooked meals every day, catering for all dietary needs and personal preferences.

🎭
Activities Programme

A rich weekly programme — music, movement, keep-fit, quizzes, bingo, seasonal parties, and community outings.

✂️
Hairdresser & Chiropodist

A qualified hairdresser and chiropodist visit the home weekly, ensuring residents can look and feel their very best.

🛍️
Mobile Shop

Our weekly internal mobile shop provides residents with easy access to sweets, toiletries, and everyday essentials.

📖
Books, Games & Newspapers

A selection of large-print books, board games, and daily newspapers available for residents to enjoy at their leisure.

💻
Video Conferencing

We provide video conferencing facilities so residents can visually connect with family and friends who cannot visit in person.

🙏
Religious Support

We support all religious and cultural activities. Holy Communion is held regularly, and Church in Wales services are arranged as needed.

👨‍👩‍👧
Open Visiting Policy

Families are warmly welcomed to visit at any time. We always offer refreshments and have a private lounge available for personal visits.

🏥
Respite & Convalescence

Short-term and respite placements available for those recovering from hospital stays or providing relief for family carers.

Not Sure What Care You Need?

Our experienced care advisors will listen carefully and help you identify the right type of care for your loved one — with no pressure and no rush.
