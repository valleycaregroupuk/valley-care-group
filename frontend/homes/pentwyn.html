About Ty Pentwyn

Nursing & residential care in the Rhondda

Ty Pentwyn Nursing Home on Pentwyn Road, Treorchy is registered for up to 35 service users. We provide residential care and dementia residential care, with nurse-led support for older people and those with dementia, learning disability or autism, physical disability, and sensory impairment — in line with our registration with Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW).

The home is operated by Quality Care (Surrey) Ltd (privately owned). Susan Rosser is the Registered Manager. The local authority is Rhondda Cynon Taff County Borough Council.

For telephone contact and the latest vacancies, please use our carehome.co.uk profile or email managertypentwyn@outlook.com.

      Frequently asked questions

      What types of care is Ty Pentwyn registered for?
      Older Person Care, Dementia, Learning Disability / Autism, Physical Disability & Visual / Hearing Impairment are the registered categories with Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW) — the regulator for Wales (not the CQC, which applies in England).
      How many rooms does Ty Pentwyn have?
      There are 33 single rooms and 1 couples/companion room.
      What are the facilities & services?
      Own Furniture if required, Pet Friendly (or by arrangement), Lift, Wheelchair Access, Gardens, Residents Kitchenette, Phone Point in own room, Television point in own room & Residents Internet Access — see the Facilities tab for the full list.
      How far is Ty Pentwyn to the centre of Treorchy?
      Approximately 0.7 mile(s) as the crow flies — distance by road may be significantly further.
