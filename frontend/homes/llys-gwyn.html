About Llys Gwyn

Care with Passion

Llys Gwyn Residential Home is a 31-capacity home operated by Grayson Enterprises Ltd. The service is personalised and second to none, with freshly cooked meals and lots of activity provided in a homely environment.

Philosophy: care with passion.

Our dedicated team maintains strong continuity of care. We are fully CIW registered — see the latest inspection themes in the panel opposite (8 Oct 2025).

      Frequently Asked Questions

      What types of care is Llys Gwyn registered for?
      Older Person Care, Dementia, Mental Health Condition & Physical Disability are the registered categories with Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW) — the regulator for Wales (not the CQC, which applies in England).
      How many rooms does Llys Gwyn have?
      There are 27 single rooms and 2 couples/companion rooms.
      What are the facilities & services?
      Own Furniture if required, Pet Friendly (or by arrangement), Smoking not permitted, Close to Local shops, Near Public Transport, Stairlift, Wheelchair Access, Gardens, Bar/Café, Phone Point in own room, Television point in own room & Residents Internet Access — see the Facilities tab for the full list.
      How far is Llys Gwyn to the centre of Bridgend?
      Approximately 6.2 mile(s) as the crow flies — distance by road may be significantly further.
