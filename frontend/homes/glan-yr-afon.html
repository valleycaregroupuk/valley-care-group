About Glan-yr-Afon

Specialist Nursing Care in Blackwood

Glan-yr-Afon Nursing Home is registered to provide care to thirty-nine residents, welcoming long-term residential needs, as well as respite and palliative care, and also running a day care service to help local home carers.

The home is well-situated for Blackwood amenities, as well as outings to many scenic South Wales locations and with much to enjoy within the accommodation itself.

All regulatory requirements are treated seriously by carers, who aim for an efficient service that is both friendly and versatile.

Home Manager

Meet Our Manager

With over 15 years of experience in clinical nursing and elderly care, our manager leads a dedicated team focused on dignity, respect, and personalized support for every resident at Glan-yr-Afon.
