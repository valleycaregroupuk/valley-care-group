About Glan-yr-Afon

Specialist Nursing Care in Blackwood

Glan-yr-Afon Nursing Home is registered to provide care to thirty-nine residents, welcoming long-term residential needs, as well as respite and palliative care, and also running a day care service to help local home carers.

The home is well-situated for Blackwood amenities, as well as outings to many scenic South Wales locations and with much to enjoy within the accommodation itself.

All regulatory requirements are treated seriously by carers, who aim for an efficient service that is both friendly and versatile.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Find answers to common questions about care and life at Glan-yr-Afon.

      What types of Care is Glan-yr-Afon registered for?
      Older Person Care, Physical Disability & Visual / Hearing Impairment are the registered categories of care with Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW) — the regulator for social care in Wales (not the CQC, which applies in England).
      How many Rooms does Glan-yr-Afon have?
      There are 39 single rooms (maximum 39 service users).
      What are the Facilities & Services at Glan-yr-Afon?
      Own Furniture if required, Close to Local shops, Near Public Transport, Lift, Wheelchair Access, Gardens, Phone Point in own room, Television point in own room & Residents Internet Access are some of the facilities and services.
      How far is Glan-yr-Afon to the centre of Blackwood?
      Approximately 1.3 mile(s) as the crow flies to the centre of Blackwood — distance by road may be significantly further.
      What is the Regulatory Rating for Glan-yr-Afon?
      Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW) is responsible for the registration and inspection of social care services in Wales, including Glan-yr-Afon. We treat all regulatory requirements with the utmost seriousness to ensure a safe, efficient, and versatile service. View Glan-yr-Afon on the CIW directory →
